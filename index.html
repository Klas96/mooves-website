About Mooves

Mooves helps you log training sessions and earn QR rewards when goals are completed. Track your running sessions, set distance and duration goals, and get rewarded with QR codes when you achieve them!

Our training log system helps you stay motivated by setting goals and earning rewards. Simply log your activities, set your targets, and watch your progress as you work towards your fitness goals.

  • Training log with distance and duration tracking
  • Set daily, weekly, or monthly goals
  • Earn QR code rewards when goals are completed
  • Health Connect integration for automatic sync
  • Goal tracking and progress monitoring
  • Beautiful, intuitive interface
